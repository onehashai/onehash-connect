# Preview your message before sending

{start_tabs}

{!start-composing.md!}

1. Write a message.

1. Click the **eye** (<i class="zulip-icon zulip-icon-preview"></i>) icon at
   the bottom of the compose box.

{end_tabs}

Click the **pencil and paper** (<i class="fa fa-edit"></i>) icon to resume editing.
